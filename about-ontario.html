About Ontario

The flag of Ontario. Image retrieved from wikipedia.com

Located just south of the Hudson Bay River and inbetween Manitoba and Quebec, Ontario is the nation's second largest province and the highest populated, holding about a third of the Canadian population. Ontario is also home to:

  • Ottawa, the nation's capital
  • Toronto, which is considered one of the most multicultural cities in the world.
  • The Great Lakes Ontario, Huron, Superior and Erie

Lake Ontario, one of the great lakes. Image retrieved from wikipedia.com

Ontario is also the most visited province in all of Canada. Being home to all kinds of sights and attractions. You can choose to go up the 500+ meter CN Tower or through the great Niagara Falls... or both! Ontario has something cool to offer everyone. Whether you are into cool architecture, nature, historical museums, art or technology, Ontario is bound to have something you will love.
